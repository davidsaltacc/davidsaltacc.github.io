Features:

A ClickGUI

Modules:

Auto Sprint

Automatically sprint instead of walking.

Spider

Get the ability to climb up walls.

Stepup

Walk on blocks without having to jump, maximal height 10 blocks.

Auto Swim

Automatically swim when in water.

Fly

Fly. Thats it. Its in the name.

Auto Fish

When fishing, automatically pull back and recast the fishing rod.

Auto Crit

Automatically land critical hits every time. For some reason that only works when standing still. (todo for me, fix!)

Anti Knockback

Don't take any knockback from attacks.

Auto Respawn

Automatically instantly respawn upon death.

Camera NoClip

In third person, allows you to move your camera into walls.

Faster Climbing

Allows you to climb ladders & etc. faster.

Airjump

Allows you to jump in the air. Hold jump to fly. If sprinting while jumping, makes you go insane speeds.

No Fall Damage

You take no fall damage. Its in the title. Sometimes glitches out when midair, but only if doing crazy movements (combined with Fly module or similar).

Parkour

Automatically jump at the very edge of blocks. (todo for me: make it so that it doesn't jump if the depth of the jump is just a block.)

Powder Snow Walk

Allows you to walk on powdered snow, just like with leather boots.

Tracers

Draws a visible line to every loaded entity. Its customizable, which entities.

Fast Break

Allows you to break blocks faster. Sometimes breaks around haste beacons.

Timer

Increases the rendering speed, allowing you to run way faster without getting teleported back. Your entire game just runs faster.

ESP

Draws an outline around entities. Again, customizable which ones.

Enderman Magnet

Looks at all endermen visible.

Special Shaders

Mojang implemented a few unused shaders. The only ones used as of my knowledge are, the spider, enderman and creeper shaders, that apply, when spectating them in spectator mode. But there is many more shaders, that can be accessed through this module.

Sneak Spam

Silly. Spams the sneak key.

Derpy

Rotates your head around like wild (visible to other players only).

No Hurt Tilt

Doesn't tilt the camera if you get hurt.

Panic

Disables all modules.

Rainbow GUI

Makes the UI instead of being black, go to rainbow.

No/Lower Fire Overlay

Lower or hide the effect when you get set on fire.

Auto Crystal Detonate

Automatically detonate (not place) crystals in your area.

BHopping

Spam the jump key, basically.

Disable Hack Client Updates

Disables the Hack Clients update cycle completely. Most modules will stop working. Can help with lag.

No Water Push

Prevents flowing water from pushing you.

Save Settings

Saves the current configuration to the settings file.

Load Settings

Loads the saved configuration from the settings file.

Show Settings Menu

Shows the hack client settings menu.

Hide Settings Menu

Hides the hack client settings menu.

Reset Settings

Erases all the settings.

Killaura

Attacks every attackable entity in your range.

Packet Logger

See what packets are being sent between you and the server. To avoid being spammed, you can filter out what you want.

Night Vision

Allows you to see in darkness.

Anti Blindness

Allows you to see, even with the blindness and darkness effects.

Anti Levitation

Stops levitation from working. Useful when in an end city and having to deal with shulkers.

No Wobble (Nausea)

Stops the screen from wobbling and warping when having the nausea effect, or inside a nether portal.

Boat Fly

Allows you to fly in boats.

Chat Commands:

sc/help

Shows a Help.

sc/panic

Disables all Modules.

+ Other Things
